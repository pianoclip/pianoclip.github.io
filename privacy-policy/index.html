PianoClip Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PianoClip ("PianoClip", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use PianoClip to create, schedule, and publish short-form videos to connected platforms such as TikTok and YouTube. By using PianoClip, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Who we are

PianoClip is an independent tool that helps a creator turn long piano performance videos into short clips and publish them to their own connected social accounts. PianoClip is operated by the individual creator who runs it and can be reached at dustin.nghiem@gmail.com.

Information PianoClip collects

  • Account connection data: When you connect a TikTok or YouTube account to PianoClip, PianoClip receives the access tokens and basic profile information (such as your username and avatar) that the platform provides through its official authorization flow.
  • Content you provide: The video files, captions, and hashtags you create or import into PianoClip in order to publish them.
  • Performance data: Public statistics about your own published videos (such as views, likes, and comments) that PianoClip retrieves to show you analytics.

How PianoClip uses information

  • To publish the clips you choose to your own connected TikTok and YouTube accounts.
  • To display analytics about your own content so you can see what performs well.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the PianoClip application.

PianoClip does not sell your personal information, and PianoClip does not use your data for advertising.

How PianoClip stores and protects data

Access tokens used to connect your accounts are stored securely on the creator's own device or server and are used only to perform the actions you request within PianoClip. PianoClip requests only the permissions it needs to publish and measure your own content.

Sharing of information

PianoClip shares information only with the platforms you connect (for example, sending a video and caption to TikTok when you publish). PianoClip does not share your information with any other third parties except where required by law.

Your choices and control

You can disconnect your TikTok or YouTube account from PianoClip at any time, which revokes PianoClip's access. You may also request that PianoClip delete information associated with you by contacting us at the email below.

Data retention

PianoClip retains connection tokens and content only for as long as needed to provide the service, and deletes them when you disconnect an account or request deletion.

Children's privacy

PianoClip is not directed to children under 13, and PianoClip does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

PianoClip may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or about PianoClip can be sent to dustin.nghiem@gmail.com.
