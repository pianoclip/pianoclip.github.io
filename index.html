How PianoClip works

From a raw recording to published clips in four steps.

1

Add a video

Import a full piano performance recording into PianoClip.

2

Clip & caption

PianoClip cuts vertical highlights and writes captions and hashtags for each.

3

Publish

Clips are published to YouTube Shorts and TikTok on your schedule.

4

Track & repeat

Analytics show what worked, and the top clips are surfaced to repost.

Built to publish on TikTok

PianoClip connects to your own TikTok account to publish your clips for you.

  • Securely connect your TikTok account with your permission through TikTok's official login.
  • PianoClip prepares each clip with a caption and publishes it to your TikTok profile on your behalf.
  • You stay in control — connect or disconnect PianoClip from your TikTok account at any time.
  • PianoClip only accesses what it needs to publish and measure your own clips.
